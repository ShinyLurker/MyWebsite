2015 CCS 11-14 All-Star Camp

Available Form/Document:
2015 CCS All-Star Camp Application Packet

CCS presents our first ever 11-14 All-Star “Catch the Spirit” Camp: April 18-19 in Clovis. This camp will include 2 long course training sessions, team building sessions, nutrition and visualization education, and a motivational speech and technique session with 3 time Olympian and Gold Medalist, Peter Vanderkaay!

Athletes must apply by Tuesday, March 3. The cost is only $100, which includes overnight lodging, in-town transportation, and food. Selected athletes will be notified by Saturday, April 4. Please read the application packet (which is available to download at the top of this column) for more details. Contact Camp Coordinator, Mark Bennett with your questions: coachmark76@gmail.com.

The fax number on the application is incorrect. The correct fax number is 559.327.2956. The application deadline will be extended to Thursday, March 5 to accommodate any applications that were not accepted via fax on the deadline. You may fax at the number above, overnight mail to the address on the application (no signature required, please), or scan and email the application to: coachmark76@gmail.com

2014 Swimmers of the Year

Congratulations to the following CCS Swimmers for attaining the 2014 Central California Swimming Swimmer of the Year Award. These swimmers are to be commended for their accomplishments! In a very competitive year, these athletes were able to accumulate the best Top 5 finishes, within CCS, to be awarded the title of Swimmer of the Year. Again congratulations to all the swimmers and ,when you see them on a pool deck, please tell these exemplary athletes "great job". Everyone in CCS is very proud!
