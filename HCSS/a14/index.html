Specialties

  • George's Special...............$7.95
    • We blend this classic with our famous vanilla ice cream, a few squirts of chocolate syrup, a heaping teaspoon of ground walnuts and three quarters of a fresh banana. Thick or thin - the choice is yours.
  • Black & White....................$6.99
    • We start a tall sundae glass and one large scoop of vanilla ice cream topped with Dewar's scrumptious hot fudge. Next we add a large scoop of chocolate ice cream and yummy whipped marshmallow. Strawberry lovers try a Red & White and caramel lovers try a Tan & White, or order a Black & Tan with Hot Fudge and Caramel. Peanut Butter lovers will enjoy our Black & Gold with liquid gold peanut butter topping. Your choice of nuts.
  • Shakes & Malts.................$6.99
    • Our shakes and malts are made with three and a half large scoops of any flavor of ice cream or ice milk. Our malts are made the same way, but with the addition of old fashioned malt powder. We mix them both with fresh whole milk to a thick consistency that you will savor until your next visit.
  • Strawberry Special............$7.95
    • A light and fruity taste makes this shake one of our most popular. It's made with plenty of vanilla ice cream, strawberries, a heaping teaspoon of ground almonds and three quarters of a banana.
  • Freezes.............................$6.99
    • Our freezes are sure to take the heat off any summer day in Bakersfield. You select the ice cream or ice milk of your choice and we'll mix three and a half scoops with carbonated water or have it with Coke or Root Beer.
  • Soda..................................$6.30
    • A century old Dewar's tradition, our ice cream sodas are a delicious mixture of vanilla ice cream, your choice of flavoring and a squirt of carbonated water.
  • Floats................................$4.49
    • Just like the ones Mom used to make. Your Dewar's server will add your choice of Coke or Root Beer to one scoop of vanilla ice cream.
  • Banana Split.....................$9.25
    • Our traditional banana split includes one scoop of Vanilla, one scoop of Strawberry & one scoop of Chocolate ice cream. Top your vanilla ice cream with hot fudge, top your strawberry ice cream with strawberries and your chocolate ice cream with marshmallow & nuts.
  • Grandpa Jim's..................$15.49
    • This banana split is made for two; with six scoops of ice cream, two vanilla, two strawberry and two chocolate, each one with your choice of topping - finished with your choice of nuts.
