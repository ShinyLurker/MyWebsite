The Kern County Chapter of California Turtle and Tortoise Club is a non-profit 501-c organization devoted to educating people on the health, care, maintenance and well being of all turtles and tortoises.
By no means should any one information system be the sole source for the care and maintenance of an animal. Several sources should be used to help determin the most accurate and healthy way to maintain a species.
The information contained in the links to care sheets is, by group consensus, the best information that is available at this time. Should you have any questions or comments on information contained on our site, please feel free to e-mail us.
The care sheet presented via www.donsdeserttortoises.com is the Desert Tortoise Care sheet we hand out in Kern County with adoptions.
