Membership

For membership, Membership Application
(Fill out form, print, sign, mail with required fees to addresses listed on form)
Fees for membership effective 1 November, 2010 are as follows:
  • $25.00 annually in the Continental U.S.A.
  • $25.00 Canada/Mexico
  • $40.00 Foreign
  • $500.00 Lifetime Membership
Membership includes:
A subscription to the Tortuga Gazette and the KernCTTC monthly newsletter, as well as discounts at selected merchants.
After you become a registered member, you will receive a membership card that will have your name and expiration date on the front to show that you are an active member in the club.
We are also looking for pet stores, feed stores, Veterinarians etc. that would be willing to provide a discout to club members who show this memmbership card when they make their purchase. So if any of you know or have time to ask businesses if they would be willing to provide discounts to a club member, please e-mail us so we can add for member use.
